# coding-project-templa  
Project-Title: e-plantShopping 

Overview:
The Paradise Nursery project is an online plant shopping application where users can explore different houseplants and purchase them through a simple shopping cart system. The application is designed to provide a smooth and user-friendly shopping experience for indoor plant lovers.

The website starts with a welcoming landing page that introduces the nursery, its purpose, and encourages users to begin shopping. Users can then browse a collection of plants organized into different categories. Each plant includes details such as its image, name, and price, making it easy for users to compare and select products.

The application also includes a shopping cart feature where users can view all selected plants in one place. They can increase or decrease quantities, remove plants from the cart, view the total number of items, and see the total cost before checkout. The checkout button gives users the feel of a real e-commerce application experience.

Overall, Paradise Nursery is a simple and interactive shopping platform focused on making plant purchasing easy, organized, and visually appealing for customers.
